About Wanda Services

At Wanda Services, we are dedicated to transforming homes, expanding living spaces, and maintaining properties with the highest level of care and quality. Based in Östergötland County, we proudly serve clients across the region and beyond.

Our Industry Experience

With over a decade of hands-on experience, we have successfully transformed numerous properties, gaining professional expertise in large-scale renovations, project management, and property enhancement in both Sweden and the UK.

Our Core Values

  • Quality: We use high-quality materials and uphold the highest standards of craftsmanship.
  • Integrity: We believe in transparent communication and honest partnership with our clients.
  • Client-Focused: Your vision is our priority. We work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.
